DSE Analyst — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 July 2026

DSE Analyst ("the app") provides educational analysis of public Dhaka Stock Exchange (DSE) data. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with data.

Data we collect

None. The app has no user accounts and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Data stored on your device

  • Watchlist and portfolio (tickers, quantities, buy prices) you enter are stored only on your device (local app storage) and are never sent to us or any third party.
  • App settings (e.g. notification preferences). You can erase this anytime via Settings → "Clear my data".

Network use

The app downloads public market data (analysis files and end-of-day / latest prices) over the internet to display the guide and charts. These requests contain no personal data. No data about you is uploaded.

Notifications

With your permission, the app may show notifications about data updates or changes to stocks on your watchlist. These are generated on your device; no personal data leaves the device.

Children

The app is intended for a general adult audience and is not directed at children.

Not investment advice

All content is educational analysis of public data and is not investment advice or a recommendation to buy or sell any security. Investing carries risk; decisions are your own.

Changes

We may update this policy; the effective date above will change accordingly.
